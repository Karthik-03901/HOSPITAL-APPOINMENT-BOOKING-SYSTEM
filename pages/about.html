About Us

Revolutionizing Healthcare
One Queue at a Time

MediQueue is a next-generation hospital management system designed to eliminate wait times, improve patient satisfaction, and streamline healthcare operations.

Our Mission

Making Healthcare Accessible and Efficient

We believe that no patient should spend hours in a waiting room, uncertain about when they'll be seen. Our mission is to bring transparency, efficiency, and dignity to the healthcare experience.

Founded in 2024, MediQueue was born from a simple observation: hospitals and clinics need better tools to manage patient flow. We've built a platform that combines real-time queue tracking, smart scheduling, and automated workflows to create a seamless experience for patients and healthcare providers alike.
